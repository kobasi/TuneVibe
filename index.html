    What is TuneVibe?

    A dynamic, multi-platform karaoke game that brings hip-hop and R&B music to life by merging advanced technology with hip-hop culture. Hip-hop and R&B are responsible for 25.1% of all music consumption. We meet the demand for these popular music genres with our digital karaoke experience.
